How does WebsiteRevive actually work?
+

You paste your URL and our AI runs a full technical and visual audit. It then generates a completely optimized version of your site — fixing performance, SEO, design, and conversion issues automatically. You review the output, approve it, and go live.

Will it break my existing website?
+

Never. WebsiteRevive generates a new version in a preview environment. Your live site is untouched until you explicitly choose to publish. You can review, compare, and adjust everything before anything goes live.

Do I need technical skills to use this?
+

Zero. If you can copy and paste a URL, you can use WebsiteRevive. The audit, redesign, optimization, and export are all fully automated. For those who want to dive deeper, we offer code access too.

What platforms do you support?
+

We support all major platforms: Shopify, WordPress, Webflow, Squarespace, Framer, Wix, and custom HTML/CSS sites. We can also export clean production code that works anywhere.

How long does the audit and redesign take?
+

The full audit takes under 60 seconds. The AI redesign typically takes 2–4 minutes depending on site complexity. You're reviewing your revived site in under 5 minutes from paste to preview.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
+

Yes, absolutely. No lock-ins, no cancellation fees. Cancel any time from your account settings and your subscription ends at the end of your billing period. Your exported code is yours to keep regardless.
